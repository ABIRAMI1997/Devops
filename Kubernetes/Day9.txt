Volume mounts, secrets , configmap

create configmap.yml

apiVersion: v1
kind: ConfigMap
metadata:
  name: app-config
data:
  APP_MODE: "production"
  LOG_LEVEL: "debug"

Use it in pod creation:
env:
    - name: APP_MODE
      valueFrom:
        configMapKeyRef:
          name: app-config
          key: APP_MODE
    - name: LOG_LEVEL
      valueFrom:
        configMapKeyRef:
          name: app-config
          key: LOG_LEVEL

Volumemounts
Create a volume and attach it pod.
